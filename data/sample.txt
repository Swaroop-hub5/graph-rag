DIGITAL CITIZENSHIP ACT OF ESTONIA (2025)

SECTION 1: E-RESIDENCY EXPANSION
1.1. The E-Residency Board (hereinafter "The Board") is authorized to grant digital identity status to non-residents.
1.2. All applicants must submit biometric data to the Police and Border Guard Board.
1.3. E-Residents are eligible to register companies in Estonia within 15 minutes via the Company Registration Portal.

SECTION 2: DIGITAL NOMAD TAXATION
2.1. Digital Nomads residing in Estonia for less than 183 days are exempt from local income tax if their primary employer is foreign.
2.2. This exemption does not apply to social tax contributions, which must be paid to the Estonian Tax and Customs Board.
2.3. Clause 2.1 is void if the Digital Nomad performs work for a local Estonian entity.

SECTION 3: AI SAFETY COMPLIANCE
3.1. All AI systems deployed by the government must adhere to the Nortal Safety Standard.
3.2. The Data Protection Inspectorate monitors compliance with Section 3.1.
